The newly built Audi R8-S
"Born on the track. Built for the road."

The Audi R8 is a high-performance sports car known for its sleek design, powerful V10 engine, and advanced Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Launched in 2006, it combines race-inspired technology with luxurious comfort, offering exceptional handling and speed that appeals to both track enthusiasts and everyday drivers. The R8 stands out for its aluminum space frame, aerodynamic design, and precise engineering, reflecting Audi's dedication to innovation and performance.

Racing teams equipped with the new Audi race car dominated the championship, showcasing Audi's engineering excellence and performance-driven design. The new Audi’s advanced aerodynamics, powerful engine, and precise handling gave racers a clear edge on the track, allowing them to consistently outpace the competition. With each lap, drivers pushed the car to its limits, capitalizing on its stability and speed through challenging turns and straightaways. Audi's relentless pursuit of racing innovation proved decisive, as the team clinched victory, securing a championship win and solidifying Audi’s status in motorsport legends.

Dare to drive it – if you can handle it.

Powered by V10 engine, delivering an astounding 562 to 602 horsepower, depending on the model. This beastly engine propels from 0 to 60 mph in as little as 3.1 seconds, with a top speed of up to 205 mph. The R8’s lightning-fast acceleration, coupled with its Quattro all-wheel-drive system, provides unbeatable traction and precision handling, making it a high-performance machine built to thrill at any speed.
